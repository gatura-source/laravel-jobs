Laravel Jobs

A Simple Job Board Built with Laravel

Simon Gatura
github.com/gatura-source/laravel-jobs

The Problem

  • Job seekers struggle to find targeted job listings
  • Many job board platforms are expensive, closed, or overly complex
  • Small businesses and communities lack simple, self-hosted solutions

Our Solution

Laravel Jobs is a lightweight, open-source job board built with Laravel for easy customization and deployment.

Perfect for niche boards, developer communities, and internal hiring tools.

Key Features

  • Post and browse job listings with ease
  • Search jobs by keyword or location
  • Admin moderation support (optional)
  • Simple, clean Blade templates
  • Easily extendable (Stripe, categories, etc.)
